Smart Deliveries

Improve productivity, increase revenues, and provide a great client experience at scale by centralising management of all your delivery activities on a single platform.

GAPP Finder Process

Make sure your deliveries arrive on schedule by optimising routes in real time and tracking them from beginning to end.

Order Accepted

After the store owner packs the order, the delivery agent receives notification to accept the order, and after the delivery agent accepts the order, the store owner receives notification that the delivery agent is on its way to pick up the order.

Order Picked-Up

After the order has been picked up by the delivery agent, both the customer and the delivery agent manager will receive an email confirming that the order has been picked-up.

Order On The Way

Allow your customers to track the rider's location in real time using geo-location on their customer app, and allow your delivery agent management to track the rider's location in real time on their dashboard.

Order Delivered

Delivery agents can keep track of how many orders they delivered, how much money they received, and how much money they earned from the deliveries they made once the order has been delivered.

  • Integration with Customer App

    Allow your customers to track the status of their order on a map interface, which includes the real-time location of the delivery agent, will increase customer satisfaction.

  • Agent App

    Manually or Auto-assigning the delivery duty to agents who are available and close by helps you save time and money. The delivery agent must be directed to make each customer's doorstep delivery as quickly as possible using the most efficient route available.

  • Manager Dashboard

    Manage the details of orders placed at online stores, as well as the amount of money that each agent has received. With powerful geo-analytics tools, you can keep track of your on-field personnel in real time, resulting in increased workforce productivity.

  • Push Notifications

    When an order is accepted, picked up, and delivered, your customers, delivery agents, and managers receive push notifications in real time.
